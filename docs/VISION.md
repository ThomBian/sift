<!-- # Vision & Narrative: The Sifted World -->

## The Problem: "Cognitive Drag"
Modern productivity tools are "infinite containers" that encourage digital hoarding. The friction of recording a thought breaks flow, and the resulting "infinite backlog" creates a "Shame List" that triggers anxiety rather than action.

## The Solution: The Secondary Pre-Frontal Cortex
Sift is a "conveyor belt," not a bucket. 
- **In-Context Capture:** Record thoughts where they happen (in the browser) via the "Courier" extension.
- **Enforced Intentionality:** Every task requires a 'Working Date' (Intent).
- **The Volatile Inbox:** Unscheduled thoughts expire in 48 hours to prevent hoarding.

## The Long-Term Goal: Unburdened Presence
Sift aims to create a world where deep work is the default. When the interface between a neuron firing and a digital commitment is near-zero, the human mind is freed from the "loop" of remembering, leading to mental clarity and reduced global anxiety.
